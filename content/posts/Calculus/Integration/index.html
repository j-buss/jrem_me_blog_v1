Definition

  • Definite Integral: Suppose \(\fx\) is continuous on \([a,b]\). Divide \([a,b]\) into \(n\) subintervals of width \(\Delta x\) and choose a point \(x_i^*\) from each interval. Then \(\int_a^b \fx dx = \displaystyle{\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}} \sum_{i=1}^n f(x_i^*) \Delta x\)
